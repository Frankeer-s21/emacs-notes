Emacs: Animation in presentations

September 28th, 2006 - http://sachachua.com/blog/p/3881

… and because this is just so endearingly old-school and crazily Emacs, here's what's going to be my title “slide” for DemoCamp10. 

(progn
  (set-face-attribute 'default nil :height 700)
  (delete-other-windows)
  (sit-for 1)
  (animate-sequence (list "Livin' la Vida Emacs" "DemoCamp10" "Sacha Chua") 1))

I may end up writing a presentation mode if there isn't one yet. I've seen one before, but I don't know if it accepts arbitrary Lisp expressions. Maybe I can mess around with eev, too..
